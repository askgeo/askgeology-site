I am interested in ecohydrogeology, or the hydrologic and landscape drivers of ecological change under the pressures of a warming climate and changing land use.

So far, I have completed a B.S. in Geology and an M.S. in Geology with a focus on hydrology. I have also worked in the public sector in wastewater permitting and Underground Injection Control at the State level.

Currently, I am pursuing research in changing soil carbon cycles, as increased drought, precipitation, and thaw are changing the rates of carbon retention or release into streams and lakes.
